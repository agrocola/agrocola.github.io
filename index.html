    About Us

    AgroCola Club is founded binding together the people highly interested in the field of Biotechnology. Biotechnology is a technology based on biology, involving living systems and organisms to develop or make products. Here, at our club we learn, practice and discuss about the latest use of biotechnology in its broad areas.

    Our Mission

    As we founded the club, our goal is to bind passionate people together in the field Biotechnology. Our mission is to create the platform of those determinant people, who always keep the Geist to do anything good but in some aspect it's hard to do something alone. Our mission is to gather those ambitious people together so they can work together and make the things possible.

    Our Plan

    We created the platform as well as some opportunity like- research fields, well arranged laboratories and experts support to any innovative ideas and teach the curious minds. We sit for club meeting every week and there we discuss and shares the current tech topic not only on biological science but also on the other tech-talks. Thus we keep ourselves up to date to the world.

    Our Vision

    Our vison is to work in the fields of molecular biology, bio-engineering, biomedical engineering, molecular engineering and some diverse sciences such as genomics, recombinant gene techniques, applied immunology, and diagnostic tests. And thus we want to contribute to our agricultural, medicinal, environmental and industrial sectors.
